FAQ's

  • How does pickup and delivery work?

    1. Schedule Your Pickup – Choose a convenient time for us to collect your laundry, either through your building’s doorman or directly from your home.


    2. We Collect Your Items – Our trained team picks up your garments with care and ensures they are safely transported to our cleaning facility.


    3. Professional Cleaning – Your items are expertly cleaned, pressed, and folded using our trusted garment care process.


    4. Fast Delivery – We return your laundry to your door or building, ready to wear, on the day and time you choose.

  • What’s the usual turnaround time?

    Most laundry and dry cleaning orders are returned within 48 hours. We’ll notify you if an item needs extra time.

  • Can you follow special instructions?

    Yes! Please leave any special instructions you may have, regarding your garments, in the 'Order Notes' section of your order in the app.

  • Do you handle delicate or designer items?

    Yes! Easy Clean is fully equipped to handle your most delicate and valuable items with professional care. Each item is thoroughly inspected prior to the cleaning process and we will reach out to you if there are any problems with your garment.

  • What if I’m not satisfied with the result?

    We’ll re-clean the item at no charge. Report the issue (with a photo if possible) within 24 hours of delivery. Easy Clean strives to do our best and treat all garments with the utmost care. 

  • How do I place my first order?

    Place your items in any disposable bag you have available. Include a piece of paper with your name and phone number inside the bag. After your first order, your items will be returned in an Easy Clean branded laundry bag for future use.
